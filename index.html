Housing Market

With the increasing prices of the housing market and increase in rent it is important to see trends in prices to be prepared for the future. We analyzed previous rent data of California counties to predict this year's predicted rent. We also provided income data for each county to show how income has varied with increase in rent.

Rent Prices

Plotted rent prices for each county from 2006 - 2021 for 0 bedrooms to 4 bedroom apartments. Gave a approximate prediction for 2022 prices and displays table for visualization.

Income Data

Provides visualization using Tableau of income data for each race in each county from the year 2013 - 2019.
